# Shot types
The list below shows all available shot types and their coresponding values.

- 0 - no effects
- 1 - bouncing (projectiles only)
- 2 - explosion
- 3 - bouncing + explosion
- 4 - homing (projectiles only)
- 5 - bouncing + homing
- 6 - explosion + homing
- 7 - bouncing + explosion + homing
